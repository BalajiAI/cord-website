About

FCC & PCI presents Re-Imagining Markets Datathon @ GFF in collaboration with SEBI, an online Datathon organised as a part of the Global Fintech Fest. It focuses on finding insights in market related data through innovative analysis using modern technologies such as artificial intelligence, machine learning and big data visualisation.

The Datathon powered by Hack2skill, aims at providing a solution for the following finance related issues:

  • Visualisation of Performance
  • Forecast trends﻿

The winners may be considered by SEBI, for suitable associations in some of their projects/committees/working groups etc.

Themes

Visualisation of Performance

Looking for a working prototype in the form of an analytical dashboard to visualise performance of a stock/outliers.

Forecast trends

Looking for a working prototype in the form of an algorithm that can predict the financial future trends, based on historical data.

Problem Statement in detail

Visualisation of Performance

Portfolio management is a complex and strategic financial function and it is extremely essential to study an individual’s preferences towards stock selection. Manually keeping a record of the market trends can be a tedious task for people involved in financial analysis.

FCC & PCI are looking for a working prototype in the form of an analytical dashboard, that can be used as a smart tool for both independent investors as well as financial advisors. The prototype must be in accordance with the following objectives:

-   Recognition of market trends and outlier stocks: Making the hunt for blue chip stocks and the knowledge of well performing companies simpler.

-   The Dashboard must take into consideration the use of modern technologies such as machine learning, artificial intelligence in order to determine and predict the significant changes that could occur in the price of a stock.

-   The analysis of market trends through the dashboard ought to be done in a graphical format making it easy for not just financial advisors and independent investors but also for inexperienced individuals who are new to the world of stock market. 

Problem Statement in detail

Forecast trends

Research shows that a lot of independent investors are constantly reviewing the past pricing history of a stock and are using it to influence their future investment decisions. This is an antithesis of the theory that the investors must only be taking into consideration the current price of the investment they own or plan to own, and its future selling price.

FCC & PCI are looking for a working prototype in the form of an algorithm that can predict the financial future trends, based on historical data.

For example:

  • Financial trends to be taken into consideration can be equities, mutual funds, commodities etc.
  • The two key technologies that are in the creation of the algorithm are Artificial Intelligence and Machine learning. 
  • Link for more financial attributes: https://www.sebi.gov.in/Curation_Links_for_Securities_Market_Data.html

F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Who can participate in Re-Imagining Markets Datathon?

    Re-Imagining Markets Datathon is open to the Students, technologist, freelancers and professional developer community.

  • Do participants need to present a fully functional idea?

    The entry should be functional for review by the judges.

  • What is the cost of participating in Re-Imagining Markets Datathon?

    There is no participation fee.

  • What prizes will the winners get?

    The winner will get the Cash prize of Rs 1 lakh, 1st RunnerUp will get the Cash prize of Rs 50K, and 2nd RunnerUp will get the Cash prize of Rs 25K. Apart from this, they may be considered by SEBI for suitable associations in some of their projects/committees/working groups, etc.

  • Team Size Allowed and Venue?

    Minimum 1 Member - Maximum 4 Members, Online

  • If it is a team submission, does that mean all team members have to fill out the registration form separately?

    Yes, all team members have to fill out the registration form and submit the application separately.

  • How do I know my registration is confirmed and when to start the development phase?

    The organizing team will approve and confirm your participation. Shortlisted teams will receive a confirmation email.
